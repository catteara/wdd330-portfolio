Outline

Purpose

  • I went through a couple of ideas and decided I wanted to try and do a Random Loot Generator for Dungeons and Dragons. I am hoping that I will be able to customize it so the user can limit what level the items are and what is included in the loot. Do they want money? Weapons? Or magical items? Or a chance for any of the items? How many items do they want generated? These are all things I am hoping I will be able to achieve.

Audience

  • Female and Male.
  • Ages 18 to 45.
  • People that like Dungeons and Dragons (or any dungeon crawler board games or roleplay games)
  • Because of the large age range I think Facebook and Instagram Ads would be able to market the web application well.

Data Sources

  • External API- https://www.dnd5eapi.co/ or https://open5e.com/
  • A local JSON file might be needed if the API’s don’t work how I want them too
